About Me

"Zachary Laguna is a Masters student at Texas A&M University. He considers himself a forever student as he is eager to build upon his academic foundations in Computer Engineering and Mathematics in addition to staying in tune with the changing technological world around us."

Welcome to my personal website! I'm Zachary Laguna, a dedicated software engineer with a passion for tackling complex problems and creating innovative solutions. Based in Plano, Texas, I have a Bachelor's degree in Computer Engineering from Texas A&M University and a strong foundation in various programming languages and technologies, including Python, Java, C++, and more.

My objective is to secure a challenging software engineering position that leverages my skills in designing, developing, and implementing high-quality software solutions. With a commitment to excellence and a strong problem-solving mindset, I strive to make a significant impact in the tech industry.

In my free time, I've been involved in exciting projects such as the Baby AI Monitor, which uses computer vision and motion detection to create a smart baby monitor, and Real Mgmt, a full-stack application developed with a team of five, hosted on Amazon Web Services. Additionally, I led the development of Gordobot, a robot that dispenses ingredients based on user input.

Education-wise, I completed my BS in Computer Engineering at Texas A&M University, a program that covered both hardware and software engineering. During my time there, I also held a leadership role as the President of Sigma Alpha Mu, overseeing multiple committees and ensuring the organization's financial health.

I've received accolades such as the Texas A&M University Honors Program and Dean's List recognition for multiple semesters. I'm also proud to have won the Safe2save Philanthropy Prize.

This website is a glimpse into my journey, experiences, and projects. I'm excited to continue growing in the field of software engineering and contributing to the development of innovative solutions. Thank you for visiting, and I look forward to sharing more with you!
